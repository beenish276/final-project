About Us

"Haveli is a beacon of traditional Pakistani cuisine, offering a unique dining experience that blends authentic flavors with an unmatched ambiance. Nestled in the heart of the city, we bring to life the rich culinary heritage of Pakistan."

Whether you're here for our signature biryani, sizzling BBQ, or decadent desserts, each dish is prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a passion for excellence. Our restaurant is not just about food; it's about creating memories with loved ones in a warm and inviting environment.

From intimate family dinners to large celebrations, Haveli is the perfect destination to indulge in the essence of Pakistani hospitality. Join us and let us make your dining experience unforgettable.
